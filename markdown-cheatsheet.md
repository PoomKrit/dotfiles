Lists 1. Unordered Lists:
• Use -, \*, or + followed by a space.

- Item 1
- Item 2

Output:
• Item 1
• Item 2

    2.	Ordered Lists:
    •	Use numbers followed by a period.

1. Item 1

- Item 1
- Item 2

Output: 1. Item 1 2. Item 2
